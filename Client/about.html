Hi, I'm Christine. I'm a former classically trained dancer that was bred to have exceptionally small hands and feet.

I spent a good portion of my upbringing indoors, playing video games of all sorts, while training to be a broadway performer (piano, singing, acting, tap dance, gymnastics, and the like). I came to be known as MsPudding from working in the videogame industry and making silly smurf account names. Somehow this one stuck.

My greatest passions right now are in the realm of self improvement. These pursuits have led me to studying Mandarin, programming, and challenging myself to write every day. In tandem, I’ve become an avid home cook, and fitness/longevity are something I strive for both with activity and research.

I go on a lot of unusual adventures these days, and more and more I’ve realized they’re not something only the rich or privileged have within reach.

Follow me for a glimpse into the life of someone who started as a shy, socially inept weakling who was completely immersed in video games, and is now riding motorcycles, climbing rocks, and hiking up mountains.

The person I’ll be tomorrow is better than I am today, and I invite you to go with me on this journey :)
